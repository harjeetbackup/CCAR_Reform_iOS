Shacharit Torah Reading Notes

This week's portion contains the reading of the Decalogue (4:6-18). There are two sets of trope for this passage. The Taamei HaTachton are used for study and divide verses into a more manageable length. The Taamei HaElyon are used for public reading and give each commandment its own verse. We chant this section of the Torah portion according to the Taamei HaElyon. For the Taamei HaElyon, see RP 1196. For more information about the chanting of the Decalogue, and to see the two tropes set side by side, see ATC 78.
