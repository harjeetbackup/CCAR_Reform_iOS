Shabbat Shuvah

General Notes

When reciting every Kaddish, after the word "l'eilah," we add the word "u'l'eilah".

When reciting the Amidah, include appropriate additions for Shabbat Shuvah.

Deuteronomy 31:1-30 (P 1547, RP 1387, WTC 1237)

7 Aliyot

Shacharit Haftarah 

Hosea 14:2-10, Micah 7:18-20, Joel 2:15-27 (P 1634, RP 1436)

Haftarah Notes

The name "Shabbat Shuvah" is based on the first word of the reading from Hosea (Some also point to the word "Shuvah" [return] and its similarity to the word "T'shuvah" [repentance], which comes from the same root.). Congregations have different customs regarding the Haftarah on Shabbat Shuvah. Some read all three selections; others recite Hosea and either Joel or Micah (Joel when Shabbat Shuvah coincides with Parashat Haazinu and Micah when it coincides with Parashat Vayeilech).
