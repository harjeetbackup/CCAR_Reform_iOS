Pesach 1 (Yom Tov)

Shacharit
Recite the festival morning service (MT 414).
Recite the festival Amidah (MT 468) with appropriate additions for Pesach and Shabbat. When we recite the Amidah silently, we replace "mashiv haruach..." with "morid hatal" (MT 472). During the prayer leader's recitation of the Amidah, he/she recites T'filat Tal (MT 473).
Recite full Hallel (MT 560). The Hallel on page 560 is the half Hallel; congregations that would like to recite a full Hallel should add appropriate selections from Psalms 115 and 116.
We recite the Torah service for festivals (MT 494).
The Thirteen Attributes (MT 496) are recited three times at festival services; historically, however, some congregations do not recite the Thirteen Attributes on Shabbat.

Haftarah Notes
Conclude the blessing after the Haftarah (MT 372) with "m'kadeish HaShabbat v'Yisrael v'haz'manim"

Minchah
Recite festival afternoon service (MT 442)
Recite festival Amidah with appropriate additions for Pesach and Shabbat (MT 468)

Maariv
Add Yaaleh v'Yavo for Pesach to the Amidah (MT 92).
Count the Omer before Aleinu (MT 570)

Birkat HaMazon
Add Yaaleh v'Yavo for Pesach and Harachaman for Yom Tov to Birkat hamazon, as well as R'tzei and Harachaman for Shabbat.
