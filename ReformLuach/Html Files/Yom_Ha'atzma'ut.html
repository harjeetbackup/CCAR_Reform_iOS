Yom HaAtzma-ut

General Notes

Yom HaAtzma-ut is a celebration of Israel's Independence. There are several potential liturgies for this day; many include Hallel and some include a Torah reading. Mishkan T'filah includes a service for Yom HaAtzma'ut on page 538, and the Plaut Torah commentary suggests Torah and Haftarah readings.

Shacharit Torah Reading

Deuteronomy 8:1-18 (P 1387, RP 1229, WTC 1094)

or

11:8-21 (P 1407, RP 1238, WTC 1104)

or

26:1-11 (P 1508, RP 1350, WTC 1193)

or

30:1-16 (P 1540, RP 1375, WTC 1224)
