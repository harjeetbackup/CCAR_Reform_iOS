Tishah b'Av (begins at sundown)

General Notes
Before sunset, eat a s'udah mafseket. There is a custom to eat no more than one cooked item.
This is a twenty-five hour fast. The fast begins at sundown.

Ma'ariv
We recite a weekday Maariv service.
After the Amidah, chant Eichah and Kinot.
Historically, the lights of the synagogue are dimmed for this part of the service, and the congregation sits in low seats or on the floor.

The end of Eichah is chanted as follows:

  • The congregation chants 5:21 with the reader
  • The reader chants 5:22
  • The congregation chants 5:21
  • The reader repeats 5:21
