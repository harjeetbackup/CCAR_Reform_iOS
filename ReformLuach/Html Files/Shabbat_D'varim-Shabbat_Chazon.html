Haftarah Notes

This is the third of the three Haftarot of rebuke that we read between the Shivah Asar b'Tammuz and Tisha B'Av. This Haftarah should be read as follows:

1:1  Haftarah cantillation

1:2-15  Eichah cantillation

1:16-19  Haftarah cantillation

1:20-23  Eichah cantillation

1:24-27  Haftarah cantillation
