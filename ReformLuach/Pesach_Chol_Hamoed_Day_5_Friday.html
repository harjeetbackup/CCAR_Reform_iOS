Pesach 6 (Chol Hamoed)

Shacharit
Add Ya'aleh v'Yavo for Pesach to the Amidah (MT 92).
Recite Half Hallel after the Amidah (MT 560)

Shacharit Torah Reading Scroll #1 - Traditional
Numbers 9:1-14 (P 1081, RP 954, WTC 848)
4 Aliyot

Shacharit Torah Reading Scroll #1 - Alternatives
Numbers 9:1-5 (P 1081, RP 954, WTC 848)
OR
Deuteronomy 16:13-17 (P 1450, RP 1274, WTC 1133)

Shacharit Torah Reading Scroll #2
Numbers 28:19-25 (P 1211, RP 1083, WTC 978)
1 Aliyah

Mincha
Add Ya'aleh v'Yavo for Pesach to the Amidah (MT 92)

Candlelighting
Before dark, we light two candles.
We say the festival candle blessing (MT 384) but not shehecheyanu.

Ma'ariv
Recite festival Amidah with appropriate additions for Pesach (MT 470)
Count the omer before Aleinu (MT 570).
Include appropriate additions for Shabbat as indicated in the Siddur.

During Dinner
Recite the festival evening Kiddush (MT 286)
Add Ya'aleh v'Yavo for Pesach and Harachaman for Yom Tov to Birkat hamazon.
