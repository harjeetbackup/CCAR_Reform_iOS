Yom Ha'atzma'ut

General Notes

Yom Ha'atzma'ut is a celebration of Israel's Independence. There are several potential liturgies for this day; many include Hallel and some include a Torah reading. Mishkan T'fila includes a service for Yom Ha'atzma'ut on page 538, and the Plaut Torah commentary suggests Torah and Haftarah readings.
